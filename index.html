Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hollow Tags?
▼
Hollow Tags is an exciting VR gaming platform that allows you to play immersive tag games with friends in virtual reality. Experience the thrill of the chase in stunning virtual environments!
Is Hollow Tags free to play?
▼
Yes, Hollow Tags is free to play! We offer optional in-game purchases for cosmetic items and additional content to enhance your gaming experience.
Can I play Hollow Tags under the age of 13?
▼
While we recommend VR usage for ages 13 and up following industry standards, younger players may enjoy Hollow Tags with proper parental supervision and consent.
